12th International Symposium on Syrphidae

International Symposium on Syrphidae (ISS) is a regular meeting of scientists and students that take place once in two years and provides an opportunity to discuss the newest findings on hoverflies (Diptera: Syrphidae) and to present talks and posters on various topics, including phylogeny, systematics, taxonomy, faunistics, biogeography, ecology, evolution, and conservation of Syrphidae.

The next Symposium, which will be twelfth ISS, will be held in the Czech Republic, in Pr≈Øhonice castle near Prague, with accommodation in Hotel Floret, located in the immediate vicinity of the castle.
