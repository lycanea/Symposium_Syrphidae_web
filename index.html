12th International Symposium on Syrphidae

About

Dear Syrphidologists,
we would like to invite you to 12th International Symposium on Syrphidae that will take a place in Průhonice near Prague (Czech Republic), in Průhonice Castle, with accommodation in Hotel Floret, located in the immediate vicinity of the castle. The symposium will start on 2nd September 2024 (Monday) in the evening and will end on 7th September 2024 (Saturday) in the morning.
